A Journey Through The Flora And Fauna Of Puerto Madryn

Website
Puerto Madryn is a small town in Patagonia, in the province of Chubut. It is a town renowned for its biodiversity and its large number of marine animals in their natural habitats. Depending on the season, you can see whales, orcas and one of the largest colonies of penguins in the world.
